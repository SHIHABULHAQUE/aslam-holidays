Your Dream Itinerary

A suggested Plan to experience the best of Kerala

Day 1

Arrival in Kochi

Pick up from Cochin International Airport. Transfer to hotel. Evening sunset cruise.

Day 2

Munnar - The Tea Gardens

Drive to Munnar. Visit Cheeyappara Waterfalls and tea museums.

Day 3

Munnar Sightseeing

Explore Eravikulam National Park to see the Nilgiri Tahr. Visit Mattupetty Dam.

Day 4

Thekkady Wilderness

Drive to Thekkady. Boat safari in Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary.

Day 5

Aleppey Houseboat

Experience the magic of Kerala backwaters with an overnight stay in a houseboat.

Day 6

Departure

Transfer to Cochin Airport for your onward journey with beautiful memories.
